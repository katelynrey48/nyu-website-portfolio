The Duology

These two projects are a horror duology 'The Martyr' and 'The Scribe'. My favorite thing is building fantasy worlds that reflect real world issues, using horror and gothic elements to explore themes like control, and moral choice.

I purposefully left characters and places unnamed to make the stories feel universal, inviting players and readers to project their own experiences onto the world.
